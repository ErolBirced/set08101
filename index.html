Path to Doom

Chapter 1: Runaway

– – –

It burns.
You can’t help but think that it is obvious though, as you have been running way past your endurance’s limit at this point.
But you know very well that you cannot stop. You will not. You must not stop. At least not until you are far away from this God-forsaken place.
As you think of this, you turn your head to look at the house you have now left far back in the distance.
…no, I cannot slow down now.
After gritting your teeth, you start jogging down the road, disappearing under the cover of the night.

After a few hours pass of walking in a mindless direction using a metaphorical compass that only read ‘away’ to guide the way, you come across a road.
You turn your head to the left.
Darkness.
You turn your head to the right.
More darkness.
…It doesn’t really matter where you go now, but if you were to put some thought into it, where would you go?
