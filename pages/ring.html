Path to Doom

Chapter 4: Ring

Ring

You feel a voice coming from the ring, beckoning you to step closer and just slip it on your finger.
Entranced, you do exactly as the voice says.
*aaaAaaaAAaaaAAAaAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHH!!*
A harrowing howl echoes across the entire cottage, shaking your mind out of the trance and rattling your eardrums so hard you feel blood drip down your ears.
Ringing pain drifts from your ears and causes you to have a shaky vision temporarily. As soon as you regain some of it back though, what you see almost makes your heart thump right out of your chest.
Long, slimy, black hair covered the face of this white-robed figure. By power of intuition, you recognise this wraith-looking monstrosity as a woman.
As the wraith seems to just be standing there in front of you, motionless, you look a bit closer to scan the apparition.
It has greyish, dried skin like a thousand-year-old oak tree, and the face, although mostly covered by its hair, made your skin crawl.
Two dark pits as if abysses were in place where eyeballs should be...
