Path to Doom

Chapter 2: Left

Prologue

After making up your mind, you start walking down the desolate road. As you walk, you are reminded of everything that you are leaving behind.
The pain.
The horrible memories.
The trauma.
Everything that could go wrong in an upbringing went wrong, and then some. You are happy that you have finally made the decision to run off. That piece of shit place can go burn in hell for all you care.
As the anger you feel rises and starts to boil through your clothes, you don’t notice the bright, pair of flashing car lights coming at you from behind.
By the time you hear the motor right behind you, the thought of your pitch-black clothes and non-reflective clothing show up in your mind. In an attempt to be sneaky, you forgot the impact that your wardrobe will have after the escape in the middle of the night.
Too late for regrets, however.

Your body is now a mangled mess of blood and broken bones – the pain so horrendous that even gasping forces inches of broken ribs into your lungs.
You are purely focused on survival at this point, your eyes darting back and forth to see what you can do to salvage this situation.
Unfortunately, there is nothing you can do.
You don’t feel anything below your disfigured torso and your arms are pointing in cardinal directions they never should.
Blood hopelessly cascades down your mouth every time you open your lips.
