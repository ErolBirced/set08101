Path to Doom

Chapter 3: Investigate

The Beginning of the End

Suddenly, like a strike of lightning on a cloudless day, a stepladder drops from a hole in the ceiling.
You made sure to close all the windows tightly as soon as you arrived in the cabin, so you know that there isn’t a snowball-in-hell’s chance of a draught opening up the door to the attic.
Not willing to leave any corners left unchecked in the cabin that you plan to turn into your home-base for the near future, you cautiously ascend into the dark abyss.
*creak*
The stepladder below your feet looks like it has experienced a myriad of winters, as every step you take makes the woody steps squirm in high-pitched screams.
As you ascend to the top of the stepladder, you reach your hands to the walls to feel for a light switch.
The darkness in the attic is suffocating, almost as if the darkness was a mucous substance that you had to breathe in and through your lungs.
As an ominous feeling started tingling the back of your neck, you stumble forward and manage to flick a light switch installed on the floor with your right foot.
Instantly, the murky darkness retreats to any hidden corner in the room it can find, patiently waiting for the next time it can consume its surroundings again.
A bit blinded by the light, it takes you a moment to adjust your sight to observe you current environment.

The attic looks old, very old. Everything inside is caked in dust – who knows how many years if not decades have passed since it was abandoned.
You spot a desk with what appears to be a wind-up toy box on it, together with a ring, a hand-mirror, and a statue of a winged creature.
Curious, you step over closer to the desk, eager to investigate the bizarre trinkets that lay upon it, as if they were antiques displayed that needed to be taken to a museum.
Unbeknownst to you, though, the darkness hidden in the corners of the attic appears to start shifting and squirming with unbridled excitement.
